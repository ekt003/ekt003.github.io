SKYLER MONCADA, also known as GiraffeOnARaft or IDrawCatsWrong online, is a full time architectural designer and self-taught illustrator currently based on the Eastern Seaboard.

I DRAW CATS WRONG. is a project developed during the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic as a response to the artist's adoption of two cats, reaffirming the important role pets play in our families. What was originally a way to practice digital art vis-Ã -vis pet portraits in his spare time turned into a way to raise money for the CT Humane Society to put towards care for all the pets still waiting for their forever home.

50% of all profits generated in this project's shop or through private commissions are donated directly to this organization on a monthly basis, in perpetuity.
