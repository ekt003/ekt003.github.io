SUMMARY:

What Lies Beneath is an augmented reality mobile game based on the rich history of Dubrovnik, Croatia. The game was developed over a five month period by nine students participating in RIT's Games and Tourism program (myself included). The game sought to encourage tourists to stray from the traditional main streets littered with shops that peddle knick knacks and overpriced food and into the side streets which hold secret marvels of architecture, history, and magic.

THE HISTORY:

According to Croatian folk lore, there existed a form of ancient vampire hunter called the krsnik. This krsnik could assume animal forms and roam the streets after dark, waiting for the vampires, also refered to as kudlak, to appear. What Lies Beneath is played from the perspective of a young priest sent from the Vaitcan to Dubrovnik to help fight the plague of vampires that is slowly engulfing the town.
