Hey There, Cool Cat

My name is Emily. I'm a third year Game Design and Development student happily spending my days at the Rochester Institute of Technology. This portfolio site holds all of my latest and greatest creations in the fields of game design, art, and software creation.

Who Are You?

As previously mentioned, my name is Emily Turner. Turquoise is my favorite color and polar bears are my favorite animal. I love games, I love making things work, and I love concept visualization (everything from data analytics to concept art to UI/UX)

I've been doing cool tech stuff ever since I signed up for my middle school's robotics team at the age of nine years old. While my enthusiasm for STEM began there in Baltimore, I made the leap up north three years ago to fuel this passion at RIT. As a Dean's List student with a 3.85 GPA, I can safely say that I've made a good transition into the world outside of Maryland.

When I'm not in class or helping out one of sixty plus students I TA for, you can find me either in the IGM labs working on new projects or tearing up the dance floor of RIT's finest swing dancing club, the Brick City Boppers.

Are You Hireable?

Yes! Glad you asked. As part of my degree, I need to complete a co-op during the Summer of 2019. A co-op is a paid internship at a company doing something related to my major. I have already completed one co-op in the Summer of 2018 as a Design Researcher at RL Solutions in Toronto. If you have a job opportunity available that involves the creation of video games or software products, please don't hesitate to reach out!
